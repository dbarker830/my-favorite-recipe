Italian Stir Fry Directions

Whether you're a brand-new home cook or a seasoned restaurant kitchen veteran, with a little bit of practice, this fresh-tasting, easy-yet-flavorful Italian Stir Fry recipe!

Step by Step instructions

  1. Heat up pan to medium-hot
  2. Add and heat 1/2 tablespoon of extra virgin olive oil
  3. When EVOO is hot enough to move around pan freely, add in prosciutto and capicola
  4. Cook meats until they start to lightly brown
  5. Remove meats from pan, then add another 1/2 tablespoon of EVOO and heat like step 2 and 3
  6. Add chopped vegetables and occasionally stir until lightly browned, season to taste with salt and pepper
  7. When finished cooking, remove lightly browned vegetables
  8. Heat up 1 more tablespoon of Olive Oil
  9. When EVOO is hot enough, cook the rice while constantly stirring until warm, then start tossing all ingredients in the pan, toss for 1 minute or until everything looks evenly cooked and starts to get hot
  10. When the mix is hot, create a small, bare spot on the pan in the middle of the stir fry, and add 2 tablespoons of pesto
  11. Cook pesto for 30-45 seconds or until fragrant, then continue mixing the pesto into the rest of the ingredients for 1-2 more minutes or until pesto is evenly mixed
  12. Serve on your favorite plastic or glassware and enjoy!

Last minute notes

If you mess something up whether it's your first time cooking or your 1000th time cooking, don't worry! Italian Stir Fry is very easy in concept to make, but it takes time to learn how different foods and sauces interact with each other. Pro tip: buy enough ingredients to make a few different servings and play around with your ratios! Make it into your own recipe and enjoy the fruits of your labor!
